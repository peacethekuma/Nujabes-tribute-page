Nujabes

The Pioneer of Jazzy Hip Hop

Nujabes performed on the stage.

Jun Seba ( 瀬葉 淳), better known by his stage name Nujabes, was a Japanese record producer, DJ, composer and arranger.Seba produced atmospheric instrumental mixes sampling from hip hop and jazz, and released three solo studio albums: Metaphorical Music (2003), Modal Soul (2005), and Spiritual State (released posthumously in 2011).

Timeline of Nujabes' life of music

  • 1974 - Born in Tokyo, Japan
  • 1998 - Founded independent record label Hydeout Productions
  • 2003 - released compilation album Hydeout Productions 1st Collection
  • 2003 - released studio album Metaphorical Music
  • 2004 - released soundtrack album Samurai Champloo Music Record: Departure
  • 2004 - released soundtrack album Samurai Champloo Music Record: Impression
  • 2005 - released studio album Modal Soul
  • 2007 - released compilation album Hydeout Productions 2nd Collection
  • 2008 - released compilation album Modal Soul Classics
  • 2010 - Jun Seba died in a traffic collision,at age of 36.
  • 2011 - Final studio album Spiritual State released posthumously.
